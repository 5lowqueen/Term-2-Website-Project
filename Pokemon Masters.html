Pokemon Masters

Overview

Pokemon Masters is a mobile Pokemon game released on August 29 2019 for Android and IOS devices. Using the different battling mechanics, the player is able to do 3-on-3 battles, with many different 'Sync Pairs'. A 'Sync Pair', is a Pokemon and Trainer duo, for example, Kris and Totodile. The main character starts off with a Pikachu, but later on, the MC will form much better pairs with different Pokemon. The main goal of the game is to become Pasio's(an artificial island, which is completely man-made) Champion, by defeating the Pokemon Master's League (or the PML for short). In order to compete in the PML, the player(MC) must have all five Badges, given by the PML Leaders through out the Main Story. Gameplay

There are three roles: Strikers(Physical or Special), Support, or Tech. Strikers are the best to attack, Supports are the best for their defensive stats, buffs, and also, healing, and Techs are meant to cripple(poison, paralyze, ecetera) the opponent. The player is able to control their three sync pairs, as they try to battle the opponents. There are limited time events. For example, Giovanni and Mewtwo, or the most recent one, "The Strongest There Is", which is a story line(in which I cannot spoil it). The most basic way to obtain Sync Paris is through the story mode, or through scouting(Gacha), using the in game currency, gems. There is single player mode, and co-op mode in which both modes are self explanitory. The best team would be a Support sandwich, with two Supports, and one Striker (A/N: Which always worked for me UwU).

Personal Connections

I have been playing this game from its release. I love this game, because you can do 3-on-3 battles, unlike the traditional battling style, where you are limited to single or double battles. However, this game has its own flaws, and the flaw is...(*Drumroll*), the game style! I have to farm for everything to unlock a certain character's level cap or moves. The graphics are on point though, but every game has its bugs, and Pokemon Masters gives us bug compensations of gems or items as compensations. I like how DeNA and the Pokemon Company incorperated manythrow back characters from the main series. I also like how DeNA pumps out new events and characters to use, as it gets even more hype! Also, the design team normally gives us hints about what's going to happen, but mainly, the datamines get leaked, and we've already been spoiled T_T.
